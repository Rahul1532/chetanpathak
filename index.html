Rustamji Institute of Technology BSF Academy Tekanpur (475005)

Hello I Am Dr. Chetan Pathak Assistant Professor and Head of the Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) at RustamJi Institute Of Technology BSF Academy Tekanpur. With a wealth of experience spanning over two decades, I had made significant contributions to the field of wireless communication, MIMO OFDM, and the designing of RF circuits. My academic journey includes a Bachelor's degree in Electronics Engineering from Y.C.C.E, Nagpur, followed by a Master's degree in Digital Communication from MANIT Bhopal (MP).I further pursued my passion for research by obtaining a Ph.D. in Communication from IIT Roorkee,(Uttarakhand), where my thesis was focused on the characterization and mitigation of linear and non-linear distortions in six-port modulators. Throughout my tenure at RJIT, I am demonstrating exceptional expertise in teaching, research, and the handling of high-end equipment such as VNA, Signal Generator MXG, and Signal Analyzer MXA.
